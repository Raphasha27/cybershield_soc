# Kivoc Denamic Hub - Business Plan (Draft)

## Vision
Build the best neighborhood digital hub in Atteridgeville for productivity, gaming, and digital services.

## Revenue Streams
- Internet/computer time packages
- Printing/scanning services
- Gaming sessions
- Tech support and training
- Snacks and accessories

## Operations Priorities
- Maximize station uptime
- Track occupancy and session turnover
- Maintain reliable connectivity
- Monitor daily revenue and expenses

## MVP Product Scope
- Dashboard for station load and session status
- Session lifecycle tracking board
- Live operations logs
- Daily metrics overview
